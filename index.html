SYNOPSIS:

A runaway highschool boy flees his island hometown to ride a ferry to Tokyo. There, he meets a girl who seems to be able to manipulate the weather. They use her ability to help others by changing the local weather to their clients' convenience. But when their usage of this ability leads Tokyo to be enveloped in extreme weather patterns, they find out that the only way to return normalcy is for the girl to sacrifice herself to the gods.
